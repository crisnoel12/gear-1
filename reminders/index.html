Requirements for Student Permit

  • Must be at least seventeen (17) years old.
    • He/She must be physically fit to drive.
    • Original & (1) photocopy of NSO birth certificate.
    • If below eighteen (18) years of age, notarized parent consent letter and (1) valid ID of the consenting parent/guardian.
  • For Female married applicants:
    • He/She must be physically fit to drive.
    • Original & (1) photocopy of NSO birth certificate.
    • Original & (1) photocopy of NSO marriage contract.
